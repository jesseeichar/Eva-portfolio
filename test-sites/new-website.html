The Sport

Kickboxing is a dynamic combat sport that combines elements of boxing, karate, and Muay Thai. It requires exceptional physical conditioning, technical precision, and mental fortitude.

As a competitive kickboxer, I train in multiple disciplines to develop a well-rounded skill set that allows me to compete at the highest international level.

I compete in Point Fighting for speed and precision, Full Contact for power and endurance, K-1 Rules for all-round combat, and WAKO Events at World Championships level.
